
The goal is to make improved and interactive versions of the chemical abundance
maps in Hayden et al. 2015 APOGEE chemical cartography paper.

allStar-v603.fits :
  from https://data.sdss.org/sas/dr12/apogee/spectro/redux/r5/allStar-v603.fits

- 69919 red giants from APOGEE DR12
- cool (Teff < 5500 K) main-survey giants (1.0 < log(g) < 3.8) with S/N > 80
- stars flagged "Bad" removed
